Settlement Foci

Foci are positive attributes of your settlement that distinguish one place from another. When a settlement upgrades, it gains a Focus or two. You may also gain Foci through beneficial Kingdom Events.

Limit: Once a Focus has been chosen, no other settlement can choose that same Focus unless the Focus says otherwise.

Quick Reference: By Benefit

Kingdom Stability & Unrest

  • Palace — Reduce Unrest by 1 each Kingdom Turn.
  • Public Forum — Gain half Unrest from Events.
  • Monument — Reroll one critical failure per Kingdom Turn.

Army & Warfare

  • Walls — Armies begin battles fortified.
  • Castle — Armies begin battles fortified; stronger if the settlement also has Walls.

Passive Income

  • Gold Mine — Monthly gold equal to half the settlement level.
  • Mint — Monthly gold equal to the settlement level.

Item Economy

  • Marvelous Marketplace — Sell magical items at full value instead of half.
  • Bazaar — Sell non-magical items at full value instead of half.

Crafting Bonuses

  • Alchemical Lab — +3 to Craft alchemical items.
  • Magical Crafter — +3 to transfer runes and Craft magical equipment.
  • Master Blacksmith — +3 to Craft magical weapons and armor.
  • Potion Seller — +3 to Craft magical potions.

Level-Based Crafting Benefits

  • Alchemical Lab — Craft alchemical items as if 3 levels higher.
  • Magical Crafter — Craft magical equipment as if 3 levels higher.
  • Master Blacksmith — Craft magical weapons and armor as if 3 levels higher.
  • Potion Seller — Craft magical potions as if 3 levels higher.

Research & Knowledge

  • Library — +3 to research-related rolls.
  • University — +3 to all Intelligence- or Wisdom-based skill checks.

Influence & Social

  • Scenic Retreat — +3 to Influence checks with NPCs in the settlement.

Earn Income Foci

  • Arcanum Guild — Arcana, Occultism — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level
  • Artisan’s Guild — Crafting — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level
  • Caravansarai — Deception, Diplomacy — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level
  • Casino — Deception, Thievery — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level
  • Circus — Acrobatics, Performance — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level
  • Druids’ Grove — Survival, Nature — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level
  • Exorcists Extraordinaire — Intimidation, Occultism — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level
  • Farming Initiative — Athletics, Nature — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level
  • Famous Tavern — Performance, Lore — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level
  • Healing Houses — Medicine, Religion — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level
  • Hunter’s Lodge — Stealth, Survival — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level
  • Museum of the Ancient Arcane — Acrobatics, Arcana — +3 to Earn Income, Increased Max Task Level

Full Descriptions

Alchemical Lab

Requirements: Town

Masters of the alchemical sciences have taken up residence in this settlement.

  • +3 circumstance bonus to Craft alchemical items.
  • Craft as if 3 levels higher when reducing the cost of alchemical items.

Arcanum Guild

A prolific guild of wizards and other arcanists have created a tower in this settlement, performing potent magical research.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Arcana or Occultism.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Artisan’s Guild

Craftspeople of all disciplines have set up shop in this settlement and created a governing body to oversee fair wages and opportunities for all.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Crafting.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Bazaar

Requirements: City

A sprawling marketplace of merchants, traders, and craftsmen has sprung up in this settlement. Goods from across the kingdom pass through its stalls and warehouses each day.

  • If you take a month of downtime to sell a non-magical item, you receive gold equal to its full value.
  • This replaces the usual sale value of half.

Caravansarai

This settlement is a major trading hub in your kingdom, attracting caravans and traders from around the greater region.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Deception or Diplomacy.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Casino

A prolific gambling den has popped up in this settlement. Citizens from across the kingdom visit this location, hoping to strike it rich.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Deception or Thievery.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Castle

Requirements: Town

This settlement is guarded by a well-protected structure.

  • If a battle occurs in this hex, your armies begin with the fortified condition.
  • If this settlement also has Walls, the first time each army would take damage in a battle, that damage is negated.
  • Special: Multiple settlements can choose this Focus.

Circus

A troupe of performers has taken up permanent residence in this settlement, providing high-flying performances to the townsfolk.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Acrobatics or Performance.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Druids’ Grove

Masters of nature have taken up residence in this settlement, making the town bloom with greenery and attracting other outdoorsy types.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Survival or Nature.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Exorcists Extraordinaire

A group of occultists specializing in warding spirits and removing possessions has set up a guild in this settlement.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Intimidation or Occultism.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Farming Initiative

Farmers in the area have joined together to cooperatively plan their harvests and pool their resources to provide food for the whole kingdom.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Athletics or Nature.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Famous Tavern

A world-class tavern is a popular feature in this settlement, attracting talented performers and audiences hungry for their art.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Performance or a Lore skill.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Gold Mine

Requirements: Town

This settlement is built near a natural gold vein, and your kingdom reaps its bounty.

  • Each month, each PC receives passive income.
  • The amount is gold equal to half the level of the settlement.

Healing Houses

Masters of the healing arts have taken up residence in this settlement, offering their services to anyone in need of succor.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Medicine or Religion.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Hunter’s Lodge

This settlement has a famous hunting lodge, a gathering place for rangers and hunters to sell their wares and share tales of their hunts.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Stealth or Survival.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Library

Requirements: City

A repository of knowledge has arisen in this settlement.

  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to rolls related to researching while in this settlement.

Magical Crafter

Requirements: Town

A team of experienced magical artisans make their home in this settlement.

  • +3 circumstance bonus to transfer runes.
  • +3 circumstance bonus to Craft magical equipment.
  • Craft as if 3 levels higher when reducing the cost of magical equipment.

Marvelous Marketplace

Requirements: City

A bustling economy for magical items has sprung up in this settlement.

  • If you take a month of downtime to sell a magical item, you receive gold equal to its full value.
  • This replaces the usual sale value of half.

Master Blacksmith

Requirements: Town

This settlement is known for its metalworking.

  • +3 circumstance bonus to Craft magical weapons and armor.
  • Craft as if 3 levels higher when reducing the cost of magical weapons and armor.

Mint

Requirements: City

This settlement has the capabilities to produce coins unique to your kingdom.

  • Each month, each PC receives passive income.
  • The amount is gold equal to the level of the settlement.

Monument

Requirements: City

This statue or memorial reminds your citizens of the greatness of your nation, even during hard times.

  • Once per Kingdom Turn, if a character rolls a critical failure, they can reroll.
  • They must take the new result.

Museum of the Ancient Arcane

A museum dedicated to recovering ancient arcane artifacts has taken up residence in this settlement, hiring adventurers to delve into dangerous, trap-filled ruins.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Acrobatics or Arcana.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Palace

Requirements: Metropolis

This settlement is home to a grand seat of government, capable of housing political officials and visiting dignitaries.

  • At the beginning of each Kingdom Turn, automatically reduce Unrest by 1.

Potion Seller

Requirements: Town

If you’re going into battle and looking for the strongest potions, look no further than this settlement.

  • +3 circumstance bonus to Craft magical potions.
  • Craft as if 3 levels higher when reducing the cost of magical potions.

Printing Press

The advent of the printing press in this settlement makes spreading information much easier.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Diplomacy or Society.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Public Forum

Requirements: City

This settlement has a devoted area for citizens to gather and air their grievances.

  • Once per Kingdom Turn, when you would gain Unrest from an Event, you gain half as much Unrest instead.

Scenic Retreat

Requirements: Town

Whether it be a charming mountain town or a lakeside villa, this settlement is a perfect place to relax and spend time with your friends.

  • When you try to gain Influence with an NPC at this settlement, you gain a +3 circumstance bonus to any checks you make.

Temple District

This settlement has attracted a number of clergy and priests to build their places of worship here.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Religion or Society.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Thieves’ Guild

Organized crime takes place in this settlement under your watchful gaze.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Stealth or Thievery.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Training Ground

A well-equipped training ground can be found in this settlement, providing quality instruction for soldiers and other fighters.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Athletics or Intimidation.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

Training Hospital

A school for training doctors and healers has opened in this settlement, along with facilities for making medicines.

  • Applies when you Earn Income using Crafting or Medicine.
  • You can perform jobs up to your kingdom’s level.
  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to the roll.

University

Requirements: Metropolis

Your settlement is home to a center for learning and research.

  • You gain a +3 circumstance bonus to all Intelligence- or Wisdom-based skill checks while in this settlement.

Walls

A wall surrounding this settlement makes it much more defensible.

  • If a battle occurs in this hex, your armies begin with the fortified condition.
  • Special: Multiple settlements can choose this Focus.
